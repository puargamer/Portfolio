Wipeout 3 Main Menu Recreation

Wipeout 3 Main Menu Recreation

2024

Unity recreation of a menu from the classic 1999 PlayStation 1 racing game
Try it out online

Using Figma, I recreated assets and formed a mock-up to recreate the menu in Unity as faithfully as possible.

  • Used Figma to prototype/wireframe the menu and understand user flow.
  • Remade assets with Figma and Photoshop, making sure they were to scale with the final Unity product.
  • Ported assets and remade prototype in Unity with mouse/keyboard and controller support.
